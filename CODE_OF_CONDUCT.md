# Code of Conduct

Loome welcomes contributors who communicate clearly, respectfully and in good
faith.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful of different levels of experience.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Keep discussions focused on the project.
- Assume public communication may be read by future contributors.
- Report safety or abuse concerns through the project security and moderation
  channels when appropriate.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Sharing secrets, credentials, private logs or user data.
- Coordinated disruption of project discussions or governance.
- Attempts to bypass Loome's security boundaries.

Maintainers may moderate comments, issues, pull requests or participation when
behavior conflicts with this code.
